Welcome to Dr. Bob Art

Dr. Bob uses thematic images in his work. Scroll through the photos throughout his site and you will see Catahoula Cur Dogs, gators, juke joints, landscapres, mojo hands, shotgun shacks, snowball stands, & soul food meccas, combined and repeated in paintings, carvings, shrines, assemblages, decor, and installations.
Dr. Bob Art sells locally, nationally, and world-wide. Most of the art on the website as already been sold. Contact Dr. Bob today & have him make some art for YOU! Price is determined by size and complexity.
